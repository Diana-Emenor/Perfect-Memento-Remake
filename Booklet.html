The Hieda family's

returning wind

brings us Nirvana;

Is she human,

or is she from the other side?

- Reimu Hakurei

The Hieda family's

unborrowable books

with a smack of the lips

were secretly borrowed;

The way of the nightingale's cry.

- Marisa Kirisame

At the Hieda house,

even time freezes

in midwinter;

Is her history

a period of nothingness, I wonder?

- Sakuya Izayoi

The Hieda family's

omen book

of the sunrise of fantasy;

Isn't its treatment of phantoms

a bit harsh?

- Youmu Konpaku
